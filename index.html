About Digital Payments

Digital payments allow people to transfer money electronically using UPI, debit cards, net banking, and mobile wallets. They reduce the need for cash, improve safety, and connect rural communities to the digital economy.

Frequently Asked Questions

💡 What is UPI?
UPI (Unified Payments Interface) allows instant money transfers using a mobile app.
💡 Is digital payment safe?
Yes, if you use trusted apps and never share your PIN or OTP.
💡 Can rural areas access it?
Yes, with smartphones and internet, rural communities can easily use digital payments.
