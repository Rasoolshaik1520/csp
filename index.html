Digital Payment Methods

UPI

Instant money transfer using apps like Google Pay, PhonePe and Paytm.

Safety: Never share your UPI PIN.

Debit/Credit Cards

Used for ATM withdrawals, shopping and online transactions.

Safety: Do not share card details or CVV.

Net Banking

Online banking via official bank websites.

Safety: Always check for HTTPS.

Mobile Wallets

Prepaid digital wallets for quick transactions.

Safety: Enable app lock.

AEPS

Aadhaar based payment system using fingerprint authentication.

Safety: Do not share Aadhaar OTP.

Digital Safety Awareness

Never Share OTP or PIN

No bank official will ask for OTP or PIN.

Beware of Fake Calls

Do not trust unknown callers asking for account details.

Avoid Suspicious Links

Do not click unknown SMS or WhatsApp links.

Report Fraud Immediately

Call 1930 Cyber Crime Helpline if fraud occurs.
