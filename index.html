How To Make a UPI Payment

Step 1

Open your UPI app (Google Pay / PhonePe).

Step 2

Select "Send Money" and enter mobile number or scan QR code.

Step 3

Enter amount and verify receiver name carefully.

Step 4

Enter your UPI PIN and confirm payment.

Step 5

Check confirmation message after successful transaction.
